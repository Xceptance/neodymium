Frequently Asked Questions

How long will shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days for domestic orders, and 5-10 business days for international shipments. Express options are available during checkout.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns of unworn, unwashed items in original packaging within 30 days of delivery. Returns are free for domestic orders.

Are your materials sustainable?

Yes, over 90% of our products are constructed using organic cotton, recycled polyester, and certified sustainable blends. Details are listed on each product detail page.

How do I track my order?

Once shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email. You can also use our Track Orders page to look up guest orders with your Order Number and Shipping Zip Code.
