Shipping & Returns

Shipping Costs & Times

We offer free worldwide shipping on orders exceeding $150 USD (or local currency equivalent). For orders under $150, standard shipping rates apply:

  • United States / Canada: $10 USD (3-5 business days)
  • United Kingdom: £8 GBP (3-5 business days)
  • Europe (DE, PL, SE, FI): €9 EUR / local equivalent (4-7 business days)
  • Japan: ¥1,500 JPY (5-8 business days)

Easy 30-Day Returns

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return or exchange the items in their original condition (unworn, unwashed, with tags attached) within 30 days of shipment.

Please package your items securely and use the pre-paid shipping label provided in your account dashboard under order details, or contact our support team.
